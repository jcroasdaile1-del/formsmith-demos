    ❓ Help & FAQ

    Getting Started
    How do I get started with the demo? ▼
    Click "🧪 Load Demo Data" on the Dashboard tab to populate the app with sample grooming salon data. This fills in clients, pets, appointments, and transactions so you can explore every feature. You can also import your own CSV file using the "📥 Import Clients" button.
    What is Formsmith? ▼
    Formsmith builds custom web apps that replace messy Google Sheets. Every tab, field, chart, and button is tailored to your specific business — no templates, no compromises. Your data stays in Google Sheets, but your team gets a polished, easy-to-use interface.
    Importing Data
    How does the CSV import work? ▼
    Click "📥 Import Clients" and upload a CSV or TSV file. The wizard auto-maps your column headers to the app's fields (it's smart about messy headers). You can review and adjust the mappings before importing. Your existing records won't be overwritten — new data is added alongside them.
    What file formats are supported? ▼
    CSV (comma-separated) and TSV (tab-separated) files are supported. The importer automatically detects which format you're using. Excel files (.xlsx) are not directly supported — save as CSV from Excel first.
    Client Form
    How do I add a new client? ▼
    Go to the 📝 Client Form tab, make sure the form is clear (click "Clear" if needed), then fill in the client and pet details. Click "💾 Save Record" to add them to your roster.
    How do pets work for clients with multiple animals? ▼
    Each pet is its own record that shares the client's name, phone, and email. When a client has multiple pets, a pet navigator bar appears between the Client Info and Pet Details sections. Use the ◀ ▶ buttons to switch between pets, or click "＋ Add Pet" to register a new one.
    What does "UNSAVED CHANGES" mean? ▼
    When you edit any field, the app tracks that you have unsaved work. A pulsing "UNSAVED CHANGES" badge appears at the top. Click "💾 Save Record" to save, or the app will ask you to confirm before navigating away.
    Dashboard & Reports
    What are the filter pills on the dashboard? ▼
    All Clients shows everyone. Active Only filters to clients with an Active status. Needs Attention highlights clients with expired vaccinations, overdue appointments, or missing information.
    Can I click on a client in the dashboard? ▼
    Yes! Click any client row in the dashboard tables or the Clients tab to jump directly to their record in the Client Form. This works everywhere you see client names.
    Finances & Checkout
    How do I record a payment? ▼
    Load a client in the form, then click "💰 Check Out". Select the service, add any add-ons, apply discounts or tips, choose the payment method, and click "💰 Record Payment". The transaction appears in the Finances tab immediately.
    Can I edit the price sheet? ▼
    Yes — go to the 💰 Finances tab and expand the "💲 Price Sheet" section. You can rename, change prices, add new items, or remove existing ones. Changes apply immediately to all new checkouts.
    Tips
    What do the little "?" circles mean? ▼
    Hover over any ? icon next to a section header or chart title to see a quick explanation of what that section does and how to use it.
    Is this a real app or just a demo? ▼
    This is a fully interactive demo. Everything works — importing, editing, saving, checkout, charts — but data is stored in your browser's memory and will reset when you refresh. In a real Formsmith app, all data reads from and writes to your Google Sheet in real time.
    How do I back up my data? ▼
    Click "💾 Save Backup" in the top-right corner. This downloads a JSON file with all your clients, transactions, and price settings. To restore, click "📂 Load Backup" and select your backup file. Your data will be fully restored — no re-importing needed.
