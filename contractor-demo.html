❓ Help & FAQ

How do I import my existing jobs? ▼
Click "📥 Import Jobs" on the Job Form tab. Upload a CSV file with your job data — the wizard will auto-match columns and let you preview before importing.
What format does the CSV need to be? ▼
Any CSV with column headers works. Common columns include Client Name, Job Type, Address, Square Footage, Quote Amount, Status, etc. The wizard will match them for you.
How do I record a payment? ▼
Go to the Job Form tab and click "💰 Record Payment", or use the "💰 New Payment" button on the Finances tab. Enter the amount, method, and status.
Can I edit the price sheet? ▼
Yes — go to the 💰 Finances tab and expand the "💲 Price Sheet" section. You can rename, change rates, add new items, or remove existing ones.
How do backups work? ▼
Click "💾 Save Backup" to download a JSON file with all your data. Click "📂 Load Backup" to restore from a saved file. Your data stays in your browser until you back it up.
Is my data saved between visits? ▼
This demo does not persist data between browser sessions. In the full version, your data syncs with Google Sheets so nothing is ever lost.
